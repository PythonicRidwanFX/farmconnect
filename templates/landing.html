About FarmConnect

FarmConnect is a digital solution focused on transforming the agricultural supply chain. It connects farmers directly with buyers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring transparency. Farmers can list their produce, while buyers can easily discover, negotiate, and purchase products in real-time.

Key Features

🌱 Farmer Listings

Farmers can upload and manage their products with pricing and availability.

🛒 Buyer Marketplace

Buyers can browse, search, and purchase farm produce easily.

💬 Real-time Chat

Direct communication between farmers and buyers for negotiation.

📍 Location-Based Matching

Find nearby farmers or buyers for faster transactions.

📊 Market Insights

Get real-time pricing trends and demand insights.
